Plants are usually categorized as a "kingdom" of plants.
That kingdom is divided into the phylum (pl. phyla).
In turn phyla are divided into classes, which are divided into orders, which are divided into families,
Which in turn are divided into the genus (pl. genera), which are divided into species.
